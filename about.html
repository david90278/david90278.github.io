About Me

This website provides an overview of my work along with some personal opinions on topics that interest me. I am married and am a father to two children. I've worked primarily in Digital Marketing and E-Commerce since 2006 and started to learn to code in 2017.

Regarding Digital Marketing I have a very strong grasp of lifecycle marketing in the E-commerce space, primarily Email and SMS marketing. I have worked with brands like Toms, American Giant, Windsor Fashions, Sportsman's Warehouse, Doc Martens, Charming Charlie and Trish McEvoy. For coding, I have been learning JavaScript, Python, Ruby and Swift UI.

I am really interested in coding as an educational tool and much of my dev work for personal projects is geared towards helping my kids become comfortable with coding. I feel like there is a great opportunity for school-aged children to use their innate creativity to create with computers the same way they create with crayons or legos. Our local Coder Dojo doesa great job getting local kids familiar with coding by using Scratch.

I grew up in Michigan and moved to Chicago after college. I finally left the midwest in 2011 and now reside in the Los Angeles area. I've been an avid runner for my entire adult life and I consider it a good day if I can get in a run - typically anywhere from 4 - 6.5 miles tends to work out really well for me. You can follow me on Strava.
