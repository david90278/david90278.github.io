Work History

I have spent my entire career helping companies with their Digital Marketing campaigns. I have worked with large companies like Viacom and NetSuite as well as small companies with a few employees. I am strongest at Email Marketing as I have been a Digital Marketing Strategist over the past 5 years.

Prior to working as a Digital Marketing Strategist I worked for 5 years in E-Commerce Operations, working for a small mom and pop business in Chicago. It provided me with a good understanding of all aspects of running a B2C retail website. The company was also one of the first third party businesses to sell directly on Amazon.

At the very start of my career I worked for a very small division of Viacom that managed online dating programs for radio stations across the country. I was primarily involved with Account Management and also assisted with implementing marketing campaigns that ran on the airwaves.
